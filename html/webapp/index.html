About


WaveZ (c) is a Cross-Platform, Massively Multiplayer Online Video Game developed by Paul Bryden.
In WaveZ (c) you must survive against increasingly difficult waves of monsters with or without the help of your friends on Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android and Web platforms!


Please note that this game is in an active state of development and will be regularly updated. I'd be more than happy to discuss my work and the game with anyone so please do connect with me on LinkedIn or @WaveZTheGame.

Development

WaveZ (c) was originally developed as a technical demonstration of Cross Platform Development Principles. WaveZ (c) was written in Java, with a combination of Native (C++, JavaScript & WebGL) libraries for the Physics, Rendering and Websocket Implementations. The game is available in the form of a HTML5 Web App, Android & iOS Apps as well as native Linux, Windows & iOS executables
WaveZ (c) is a Massively Multiplayer Online Game and employs a client-server architectural model w/ Web Sockets as the transport layer of choice. TCP_NODELAY was utilized extensively in conjunction with a unique multi-socket implementation which enabled the emulation of UDP without the impact and interference of Nagles Algorithm or TCP Packet Loss. Physics are calculated in realtime on both the client and server and are synchronized using periodic "state of the world" frames.
Motion compensation is used extensively on the client side (and partially on the server side) to ensure the smooth transition between authoratitive updates and client-side predicted updates. Client-side prediction principles are employed to reduce bandwidth requirements and offset the impact of delay on the network.
The Game was developed by Paul Bryden in some spare time over a 1.5 month period. I hope you enjoy it and please do leave feedback @WaveZTheGame!

Currently WaveZ (c) is only available on desktop & Android.
